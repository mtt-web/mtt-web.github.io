Among my passions is book reading. Since childhood I have been reading literature. For a period I also tried fiction, but it didn't interest me much. When I am interested in fiction, I usually listen to it in the form of an audio book — that works better for me, because my audio memory for stories is stronger.
I mostly read books about programming, philosophy, psychology and other popular literature.
Below is a list of literature that I have studied. It contains my personal history of books borrowed from the scientific library and the non-scientific library (mostly from 2003 to 2008), as well as books that I bought or otherwise obtained.
