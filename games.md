In my free time, I create computer games. I use the C# programming language and the Unity game engine from Unity Technologies. While in college, I programmed school assignments in the field of computer games, a simple text adventure game, and in my free time, I programmed smaller board games (Crossword puzzle, similar to the game Lines) in WinForms and one game, a remake of the 1984 game Tapper in Unity. Since high school, I have been programming smaller logic games in C/C++, such as Tetris, Tic-Tac-Toe, and simple chess.
