The folk jazz recordings were played on an Epiphone Les Paul Standard and Tension nylon acoustic guitar.  
Jazz and blues recordings were performed on an Ibanez AF75 jazz guitar.

## Folk Jazz

As mentioned above, these compositions were recorded on the Epiphone Les Paul Standard and Tension nylon acoustic guitar using either direct input or a standard electret microphone.

The music was inspired mainly by folk-jazz musician [Karel Plíhal](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Karel_Pl%C3%ADhal){: target="_blank" }.
